What “cheat_risk” means
Cheat risk = how easy it is to get a “win” that isn’t real. High risk means a result can look successful while secretly taking an easier path.
LOW — hard to fake
MED — needs controls + replicate
HIGH — strict controls + outside verification
Use it: higher cheat risk means your first move is stronger falsifier + stricter witness order.
